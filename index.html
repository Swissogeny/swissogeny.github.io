Swissogeny Day

The swiss isogeny day is a recurring meetup of cryptographers working on isogeny-based cryptography in Switzerland or close to it.

Next event

Date: March 20, 2025
Place: IBM Research, Säumerstrasse 4, Rüschlikon, Switzerland
Travel: By train from Zurich HB: take the S24 or S8 and stop in Rüschlikon, then you can walk (uphill) 15 mins.
By bus from Bürkliplatz: take the 165 and stop in Rüschlikon, Säumerstrasse, then go for the main entrance (there will be employees entering from the secondary one).

Tentative Schedule

09:30-10:00 Welcome and coffee
10:00-11:30 Talks
11:30-13:00 Lunch
13:00-15:00 Present ideas, then brainstorm
15:00-15:30 Coffee break
15:30-17:30 Brainstorm and conclusion
