Swissogeny Day

The swiss isogeny day is a recurring meetup of cryptographers working on isogeny-based cryptography in Switzerland or close to it.

Next event

Date: March 20, 2025
Place: IBM Research, Säumerstrasse 4, Rüschlikon, Switzerland

Travel: By train from Zurich HB: Take the S24 or S8 train and stop in Rüschlikon, then you can walk (uphill) 15 mins.
By bus from Bürkliplatz: Take bus 165 and stop in Rüschlikon, Säumerstrasse, then go to the main entrance (warning: if you follow the employees you'll find yourself at a side-entrance, ask the directions to the main entrance).
When you arrive: Enter the building and, if we are not there, tell your name and ask for Giacomo Borin (shortname 'gib').
